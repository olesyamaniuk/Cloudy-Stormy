About the Game

  • Step into a world where the weather is your playground! In Cloudy & Stormy, you take control of the skies, shifting between peaceful sunshine, dramatic storms, and everything in between.
    Whether you're guiding fluffy clouds across the sky, summoning lightning bolts, or steering powerful winds, every action changes the world below. Will you create harmony or unleash chaos? The choice is yours!
  • Development Challenges:
    Realistic yet fun physics-based cloud movement to make the game both engaging and visually stunning.
    Balancing weather mechanics so that storms feel powerful yet fair.
    Creating dynamic sky environments that evolve in response to player actions.

How to Play

  • Tap & Drag Clouds

    Move the clouds across the sky to influence the weather.

  • Summon Storms

    Control the intensity of thunderstorms, rain, and wind.

  • Solve Weather Puzzles

    Complete unique challenges that test your cloud-controlling skills.

  • Unlock New Abilities

    Master new weather powers as you progress.

  • Create Your Own Sky

    Free play mode lets you experiment with different weather patterns!

Game Atmosphere

  • The world of Cloudy & Stormy is a visual and sensory delight. The soothing movement of clouds, the dramatic flashes of lightning, and the calming patter of rain create an immersive sky experience like no other.
  • With a stunning dynamic soundtrack that shifts from soft wind chimes to deep rolling thunder, every moment feels alive. Whether you want to create peaceful sunsets or raging storms, the atmosphere adapts to your every move.

    Cloudy & Stormy is available for Android and iOS devices.
